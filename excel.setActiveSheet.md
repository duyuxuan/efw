<H1>Excel.setActiveSheet</H1>

The setActiveSheet function is established a sheet to be active.

<h2>Sample</h2>
<pre>
	var excel = new Excel("test.xlsx");
	excel.createSheet("newSheet").setActiveSheet();
</pre>
